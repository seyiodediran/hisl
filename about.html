About HISL Recyclers

A Lagos based recycling company focused on promoting socio-economic and environmental healthy living through proper collection and recycling of post consumers waste such as Plastics, Beverages aluminum can, Carton, and nylon to end landfilling.

As a for-profit social enterprise, we pay attention to achieving sustainable waste management and zero waste in lagos and Nigeria as a whole by leveraging on the Lagos State Blue Box Initiative and other available smart technologies to help enhance our services and ensure our subscribers (individuals and households) are financially inclusive through their daily waste generated.

With our special buy back system, our potential subscribers get easily paid with our incentives model, based on the equivalent of volume of recyclables submitted.
